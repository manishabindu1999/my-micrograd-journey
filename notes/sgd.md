# Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)

## What is SGD?

SGD stands for:

Stochastic Gradient Descent

It is one of the simplest optimization algorithms.

---

## Core Update Rule

weight = weight - learning_rate * gradient

---

## Why SGD Matters

SGD introduced scalable neural network optimization.

It remains foundational in deep learning.

---

## Advantages

- simple
- memory efficient
- fast

---

## Disadvantages

- noisy updates
- slower convergence than Adam
