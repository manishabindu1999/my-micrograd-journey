# Adam Optimizer

## What is Adam?

Adam is one of the most widely used deep learning optimizers.

It combines:

- momentum
- adaptive learning rates

---

## Why Adam Matters

Adam trains neural networks efficiently and stably.

It works well for:

- transformers
- language models
- diffusion models

---

## Key Features

Adam adapts learning rates automatically.

It also smooths noisy gradients.

---

## Advantages

- fast convergence
- stable training
- widely effective

---

## Why Modern AI Uses Adam

Large neural networks often rely on Adam-based optimization.

Many transformer systems use Adam variants internally.
