# Learning Rate

## What is a learning rate?

The learning rate controls how large parameter updates are during training.

It determines how fast a neural network learns.

---

## Update Equation

weight = weight - learning_rate * gradient

---

## Small Learning Rate

Advantages:

- stable learning
- smoother optimization

Disadvantages:

- slow training

---

## Large Learning Rate

Advantages:

- faster updates

Disadvantages:

- unstable training
- overshooting minima

---

## Why Learning Rate Matters

The learning rate strongly affects:

- training stability
- convergence speed
- final model quality

Choosing a good learning rate is extremely important.
