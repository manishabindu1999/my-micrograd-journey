# tanh Activation Function

## What is tanh?

tanh is a nonlinear activation function.

It outputs values between:

-1 and 1

---

## Equation

tanh(x) = (e^x - e^-x) / (e^x + e^-x)

---

## Why tanh Matters

tanh introduces nonlinearity into neural networks.

It is commonly used in:

- educational neural networks
- recurrent networks
- micrograd examples

---

## Properties

Advantages:

- smooth gradients
- zero-centered outputs

Disadvantages:

- can suffer from vanishing gradients
